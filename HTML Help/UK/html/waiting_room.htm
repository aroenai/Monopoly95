Waiting Room

Would you like to play MONOPOLY CD-ROM against opponents from all over the world? After establishing your usual Internet connection, double-click on the Wchat icon. This will connect you to the Westwood Studios MONOPOLY CD-ROM game Waiting Room. Tune in to the MONOPOLY CD-ROM game channel, where you can talk to other players or challenge them to a game over the Internet!

Using your favorite web browser, you can also visit the Westwood Studios World Wide Web Site at http://www.westwood.com. From the Home Page, click on Index and select the MONOPOLY CD-ROM game Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ).
