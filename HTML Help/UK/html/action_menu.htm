Action Menu

The Action Menu is normally only available to the player whose turn it is to play. If another player wishes to access this menu, that player must interrupt play.

Mortgage properties

Select this option to mortgage any undeveloped properties which you own. Your properties will be highlighted on the board. Click on a property to mortgage it. After you have finished selecting properties to mortgage, click on Ok to confirm, or Cancel to undo.

Unmortgage properties

Select this option to redeem the mortgage any of your mortgaged properties. Your mortgaged properties will be highlighted on the board. Click on a property to unmortgage it. After you have finished redeeming mortgages, click on OK to confirm, or Cancel to undo.

Buy buildings

Select this option to build houses or hotels on any monopoly which you own. Your monopolies will be highlighted on the board. Click on a property to build a house. If you already have four houses there, you will have to build a hotel, and give the four existing houses to the bank in part payment. When you have finished putting up buildings, click on OK to confirm, or Cancel to undo.

Sell buildings

Select this option to sell houses or hotels back to the Bank. Your developed monopolies will be highlighted on the board. Click on a property to sell a building. When you have finished selling buildings, click on OK to confirm, or Cancel to undo.

Propose deal

Select this option to propose a deal to other players in the game. The dealing window will appear in the middle of the board. You may offer cash, title deeds, Get Out of Jail Free Cards, and Immunity as part of a deal. Place any items you wish to acquire in your token's trading window. Now place any items you wish to sell to other players into their tokens’ windows. The deal will go ahead if all the players involved accept the terms. Please see Proposing a Deal for additional details.

Show owners

Select this option to highlight which properties are owned, and see who owns them. All the non-vacant properties will be highlighted on the board. The token that owns a particular property is shown on the appropriate square. Click on OK when you have finished viewing the properties.
