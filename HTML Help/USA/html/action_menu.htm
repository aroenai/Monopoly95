Action Menu

The Action Menu is only available to the current player. If another player wishes to perform one of these actions, that player must interrupt play.

Mortgage properties

Select this option during your turn to mortgage any undeveloped properties which you own. Your properties will be shown on the game board. Click on a property to mortgage it. After you have finished selecting properties to mortgage, click on Okay to complete, or Cancel to undo.

Unmortgage properties

Select this option during your turn to unmortgage any mortgaged properties which you own. Your mortgaged properties will be shown on the game board. Click on a property to unmortgage it. After you have finished selecting properties to unmortgage, click on Okay to complete, or Cancel to undo.

Buy building(s)

Select this option during your turn to build houses or hotels on any monopoly which you own. Your monopolies will be shown on the game board. Click on a property to construct a house. After four houses, constructing an additional building will create a hotel. After you have finished constructing buildings, click on Okay to complete, or Cancel to undo.

Sell building(s)

Select this option during your turn to sell houses or hotels back to the Bank. Your developed monopolies will be shown on the game board. Click on a property to sell a building. After you have finished selling buildings, click on Okay to complete, or Cancel to undo.

Propose trade

Select this option during your turn to initiate a trade with other players in the game. The trade screen will appear in the middle of the game board. You may offer cash, title deeds, Get Out of Jail Free Cards, and Immunity as part of a trade. Items placed in your token's trading window are items that you will receive if the trade is accepted by all involved players. Items placed in an opponent's trading window are the items that token will receive if the trade is accepted by all involved players. Please see Proposing a Trade for additional details.

Show owners

Select this option to show which properties are owned. All of the owned properties are shown on the game board. The token that owns a particular property is shown on the appropriate game square. Click on Okay when you are finished viewing the properties.
